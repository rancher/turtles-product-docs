== Release Date: 2026-03-19

== Description

[IMPORTANT]
====
Please read below information before updating.
====

This release is a major step for Turtles and CAPI in Rancher as it ships with https://github.com/kubernetes-sigs/cluster-api/releases/[CAPI v1.12] and adopts the new core CAPI contract v1beta2. The Rancher team has put together a simplified xref:tutorials/capi-v1beta2.adoc[migration guide] to make the transition as easy as possible.

Additionally, you can refer to the https://cluster-api.sigs.k8s.io/developer/providers/migrations/v1.10-to-v1.11[official CAPI migration guide] to understand the full extent of the implications of moving to a CAPI version `>=v1.11`.

**Before updating we recommend that you familiarize with the changes introduced by your provider/s of choice and, if needed, adapt your cluster templates accordingly.**

=== Notable Changes

* Add support for CAPI v1.12 by @salasberryfin in https://github.com/rancher/turtles/pull/2097[#2097] (also worth looking at bump CAPI to v1.11.5 by @salasberryfin in https://github.com/rancher/turtles/pull/2056[#2056])
* Disable Rancher k8s version management for CAPI imported Clusters by @anmazzotti in https://github.com/rancher/turtles/pull/2011[#2011]
* Bump go to 1.25.8 by @rancher-backport-assistant[bot] in https://github.com/rancher/turtles/pull/2198[#2198]
